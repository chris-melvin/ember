Features
Everything you need,
nothing you don't

Built for people who think out loud and want to keep what they said.

Video + Audio

Record camera and mic together, or switch to audio-only from the menu bar.

Whisper models

Base, medium, or large-v3-turbo. Downloads on first launch, runs fully offline after.

Title prompts

Name recordings after you stop. Titles become filenames and transcript headings.

Recording library

Browse, search, and manage all your recordings and transcripts in one place.

In-app playback

Play audio and video right in the library. Seek bar, transport controls, transcript sync.

Full-text search

Search across titles and every word in your transcripts. Find any moment from any recording.

Edit transcripts

Fix mistakes inline. Timestamps stay locked, text is editable. Auto-saves as you type.

Tags & filters

Tag recordings, filter by date or tag. Organize naturally as your library grows.

Synced transcript

Playback highlights the active segment. Click any timestamp to jump to that moment.

Workflow
Three steps, that's it

No configuration marathons. Install, set your output folder, go.

1

Record

Press Cmd+Shift+R from any app. A floating panel appears with your camera feed and a timer.

2

Transcribe

Stop recording and whisper.cpp processes your audio on-device with Metal GPU acceleration.

3

Browse

Your recording and transcript appear in the Ember library. Play it back, search it, edit it, tag it.
