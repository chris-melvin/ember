Instant capture

One hotkey to start, one to stop. Your thoughts don't wait, and neither does Ember.

Local transcription

whisper.cpp runs on your hardware with Metal GPU acceleration. Nothing leaves your Mac.

Ready for your vault

Markdown with frontmatter and wikilinks. Drops right into Obsidian, Logseq, or any editor.

Features

Everything you need,
nothing you don't

Built for people who think out loud and want to keep what they said.

Video + Audio

Record your camera and microphone together, or switch to audio-only mode from the menu bar.

Whisper Models

Choose from base, medium, or large-v3-turbo. Models download on first launch and run offline.

Recording Library

Browse all recordings with search, status badges, and one-click Finder reveal. Delete what you don't need.

Title Prompts

Name each recording after you stop. Titles become slugified filenames and transcript headings.

Three output formats

Markdown with YAML frontmatter and timestamps, plain text for simplicity, or SRT subtitles for video workflows.

Workflow

Three steps. That's it.

Record

Hit Cmd+Shift+R from anywhere. A floating panel shows your camera and a timer.

Transcribe

whisper.cpp processes your audio locally using Metal acceleration. No upload, no wait.

Done

Your transcript appears in your vault or output folder. Open it in Obsidian, VS Code, or anything.

Record from anywhere

One shortcut, no context switching. Works in any app, any space.
