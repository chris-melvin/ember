Ember

Record your thoughts, get them transcribed locally. A macOS menu bar app for voice capture with whisper.cpp.

Download for macOS

macOS 14+ · Apple Silicon & Intel · Free & open source

Local Transcription
Powered by whisper.cpp. Your audio never leaves your machine. No API keys, no subscriptions.
Video + Audio
Record your camera and mic together, or go audio-only. Switch modes from the menu bar.
Obsidian Integration
Transcripts with YAML frontmatter and wikilinks. Point Ember at your vault and it just works.
Multiple Formats
Export as Markdown, plain text, or SRT subtitles. Timestamps included.
Global Hotkey
Cmd+Shift+R to start and stop. Record from anywhere without switching apps.
Recording Library
Browse, search, and manage all your recordings and transcripts in one place.

How it works

1
Record
Press Cmd+Shift+R or click the menu bar icon to start recording.
2
Transcribe
Ember runs whisper.cpp locally to transcribe your recording.
3
Organize
Transcripts land in your output folder, ready for Obsidian or any editor.
